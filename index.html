✦ 6-Day Perfect Plan · March 26–31 ✦

Nara & Haejoo's Dream 6 Days

1Day

Arrive Palma · Old Town & First Sunset

SightseeingDining
  • Check in, stroll the Cathedral La Seu — dramatic Gothic exterior on the waterfront
  • Wander the Arab Quarter (El Call) and Almudaina Palace gardens
  • Sunset drinks at Pacha Palma Beach rooftop overlooking the bay
  • Dinner at Adrián Quetglas — Michelin-starred, book ahead
2Day

Sóller Valley · Vintage Train & Kayaking

SightseeingWater Sports
  • Wooden vintage train Palma → Sóller (1h scenic mountain journey)
  • Hire kayaks at Port de Sóller — paddle the turquoise coves
  • Snorkelling at Cala Tuent, one of Majorca's most pristine bays
  • Sunset at Mirador de ses Barques — panoramic Tramuntana views
3Day

Private Boat Charter · Hidden Coves & Cliff Jumping

Water SportsSightseeing
  • Private boat from Port d'Andratx — sail to Sa Dragonera nature reserve island
  • Cliff jumping and swimming at crystal-clear Cala en Basset
  • Paddleboarding and floating lunch on deck with chilled rosé
  • Evening at Andratx village — local wine bar and market browsing
4Day

Shopping Day · Palma Farewell

ShoppingDining
  • Carrer de Sant Miquel & Passeig del Born — boutique fashion & design stores
  • Mercat de l'Olivar — local cheeses, sobrasada sausage, Majorcan pearls
  • Avinguda Jaume III — luxury boutiques: Hermès, Loewe, Zara flagship
  • Farewell dinner at La Bodeguilla — traditional Mallorcan cuisine and wine list
5Day

Fly to Marrakech · Medina & the Souks

SightseeingShopping
  • Morning flight Palma → Marrakech (2h 20m); check into riad
  • Explore the UNESCO Medina — souks for spices, leather goods, brass lanterns
  • Visit the Saadian Tombs and Bahia Palace — golden hour is breathtaking
  • Evening at Jemaa el-Fna square — snake charmers, musicians, street food
  • Rooftop dinner at Nomad restaurant overlooking the rooftops
6Day

Hammam Spa & Atlas Mountain Adventure

LeisureSightseeingShopping
  • Sunrise hammam & argan oil massage at Hammam de la Rose — pure luxury
  • Souk el Attarine — argan oil, Berber rugs, Moroccan perfumes (negotiate!)
  • Afternoon camel ride or quad bike into the Palmeraie palm groves
  • Optional day trip: Ourika Valley & Atlas Mountains waterfalls (1h drive)
  • Farewell feast at La Maison Arabe — live Gnaoua music & tagine dinner

✦ 6-Day Perfect Plan · March 26–31 ✦

Nara & Haejoo's Dream 6 Days

1Day

Arrive Valletta · Baroque Grandeur & Grand Harbour

SightseeingDining
  • Check in, walk the Grand Harbour — one of Europe's most dramatic views
  • St. John's Co-Cathedral: Caravaggio's masterpiece painting is inside
  • Upper Barrakka Gardens at sunset — daily 4pm cannon salute from the bastions
  • Dinner at Noni Restaurant — modern Maltese fine dining, elegant atmosphere
2Day

Blue Lagoon · Snorkelling & Sea Kayaking

Water SportsSightseeing
  • Ferry from Ċirkewwa to Comino Island (30 min) — the legendary Blue Lagoon
  • Snorkelling and swimming in crystal-turquoise waters (visibility up to 30m)
  • Sea kayaking around Comino's sea caves and natural arches
  • Optional: PADI dive at the Blue Hole cave dive, Gozo — world-famous site
  • Evening: sundowners at Trabuxu Wine Bar in Valletta
3Day

Gozo Island · Citadel & Ramla Bay

SightseeingLeisure
  • Ferry to Gozo (25 min) — hire a scooter or driver for the day
  • Dwejra Bay (former Azure Window site) — still a dramatic seascape
  • Ramla Bay — Malta's most beautiful sandy beach set in red-sand valley
  • Victoria Citadel medieval fortified city — panoramic views across Gozo
  • Lunch at Ta' Rikardu inside the Citadel — local cheese, gbejniet and wine
4Day

Ferry to Sicily · Taormina & Greek Theatre

SightseeingShopping
  • Morning Virtu Ferry Malta → Pozzallo, Sicily (90 min scenic crossing)
  • Drive to Taormina (1h) — jewel of the Sicilian coast, perched on cliffs
  • Ancient Greek Theatre of Taormina with Mt. Etna framed in the backdrop
  • Corso Umberto shopping street — handmade ceramics, Sicilian wines, jewellery
  • Dinner at Otto Geleng (Michelin-starred) with open-air terrace and sea views
5Day

Mt. Etna Hike & Isola Bella Beach

SightseeingWater Sports
  • Morning guided hike or cable car up Mt. Etna — Europe's largest active volcano
  • Lava field crater rim walk with panoramic Sicily views (weather permitting)
  • Afternoon: Isola Bella nature reserve cove — snorkelling in gin-clear water
  • Paddleboarding or windsurfing hire at Letojanni beach (5 min from Taormina)
  • Sunset cocktail at Terrazza Angelo with Etna glowing behind you
6Day

Catania Markets, Shopping & Farewell

ShoppingDining
  • La Pescheria fish market Catania — an extraordinary spectacle of colour and noise
  • Via Etnea & Piazza del Duomo — Sicilian ceramics, handmade lace, local food gifts
  • Catania's Baroque palaces and the iconic Elephant Fountain
  • Farewell cannoli and arancini at Savia — the city's most famous bakery since 1897
